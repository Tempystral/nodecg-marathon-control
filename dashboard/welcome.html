Welcome!

Thank you for installing nodecg-marathon-control! To get started, here are a couple tasks to complete the set up process.

Upload Layouts, Images and More

In the assets tab, upload all your marathon's graphics, including layouts and logos. You can even upload custom fonts to customize text!

Change The Text Position

With your layouts uploaded, use the Game Layout, Intermission and Twitch Players panel in the Settings tab to adjust the position, size, and formatting of text. You can even use custom fonts!

Set Up OBS Preview/Program

Using VDO.Ninja, create a Remote Screenshare Into OBS, select your preview/program window, copy the room code and input it into the OBS Properties panel in the Settings tab.

Select Your Intermission/Game Scenes

In the OBS Properties panel in the Settings tab, select your intermission and game scene from the dropdowns. This ensures that Auto Record and various functions work correctly!

Need Help?

No problem! A full setup and user guide, as well as troubleshooting steps, can be found on the wiki. Additional support is also available on our Discord server.
